Automatic vs Manual Wire Stripping - Which One Do You Need?

Compare both methods before choosing equipment for your production line.

Comparison Table

FactorAutomatic Wire StrippingManual Wire Stripping
SpeedHigh output for repeated batch workBetter for very small quantities
ConsistencyStable length and stripping depth after setupDepends heavily on operator skill
Labor CostLower labor per piece in batch productionLower machine cost, higher labor time
FlexibilityBest for saved settings and repeated modelsBest for repair, sample, and quick changes

Suitable Scenario Analysis

Choose automatic stripping when your factory processes the same wire sizes every day and needs stable exposed copper length.

Choose manual stripping when you only prepare occasional wires, repair prototypes, or handle irregular cables that need operator judgment.

Recommended Conclusion

For most harness workshops with daily batch production, an automatic computer wire stripping machine is the better long-term choice.
