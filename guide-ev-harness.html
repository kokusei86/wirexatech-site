Wire Processing Solutions for EV Battery Harness Production

EV battery harness production often requires larger wire capacity, clean stripping quality, and careful handling of special cable structures.

EV Harness Characteristics

EV battery harness wires often use larger cross sections and demand high precision. Poor stripping can damage conductors, shielding, or insulation layers, so machine selection should start from real wire samples.

Recommended Equipment Direction

For medium-size EV auxiliary harnesses, use a computer wire stripping and cutting machine matched to the required wire range. For heavy battery cables, confirm whether a heavy-duty cutting and stripping solution is needed.

Important Notes

Double-layer insulation, shielded cable, silicone jacket, and high-voltage wire may require special blade geometry, slower stripping speed, and sample testing before production setup.
