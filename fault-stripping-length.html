Wire Stripping Length Inaccurate - Causes and Adjustment Guide

Length problems can appear as exposed copper that is too short, too long, or unstable between pieces.

Symptom Description

The machine may cut the correct total length but produce inaccurate stripping length. In other cases, both cutting length and stripping length drift during production.

4 Causes and Adjustment Methods

01Front or rear stripping parameter is wrong

→ Fix: Check front strip, rear strip, half strip, and pull-back settings one by one.

02Wire slips during feeding

→ Fix: Adjust roller pressure and clean the roller surface so the actual feed length matches the set value.

03Blade depth is too shallow or too deep

→ Fix: Adjust blade depth until insulation separates cleanly without cutting copper strands.

04Compensation value is incorrect

→ Fix: Measure several samples, calculate average error, and adjust compensation gradually.
