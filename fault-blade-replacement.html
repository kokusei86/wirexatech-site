How to Replace Wire Stripping Blades - Complete Step-by-Step Guide

Blade condition directly affects stripping quality, copper protection, and machine stability.

When Should You Replace the Blade?

Replace the blade when insulation becomes rough, copper strands are damaged, the cut edge is uneven, or the blade has visible chips, cracks, or dull edges.

5-Step Operation Process

01Power off and clean the blade area

→ Fix: Turn off the machine and remove wire debris before touching the blade holder.

02Remove the old blade carefully

→ Fix: Loosen the blade screws evenly and keep the original installation direction for reference.

03Install the replacement blade

→ Fix: Place the new blade in the same position and tighten screws gradually to avoid offset.

04Check blade center and opening

→ Fix: Confirm that upper and lower blades meet evenly and match the conductor diameter.

05Run a small test batch

→ Fix: Test 5-10 pieces, then adjust cutting depth until stripping is clean without copper damage.
