Step 1

Trial Cut - Adjust Cutter Depth

00:00:00

Training Card

Step NameTrial Cut - Adjust Cutter Depth
Why do this?Verify data and fine-tune cutter depth to ensure proper strip depth
When is this needed?After entering a new program, changing wire, or during first trial cut
Operation Steps
  1. Press Run button to cut one piece
  2. Check both ends of the cut wire for scoring but not stripped
  3. Press Stop button to stop
  4. Press Cutter Depth key and increase the value (e.g., from 140 to 146)
  5. Press Run button again to trial cut
Success CheckStripped ends clean, no scratches, no un-stripped areas
Common Mistakes
  • Forgetting to press Stop before adjusting parameters
  • Increasing cutter depth too much, damaging the conductor
  • Adjusting too large a step and overshooting the optimal value
Key Values
  • Cutter depth 140: current parameter, unit 0.01 mm
  • Cutter depth 146: increased parameter, deeper strip depth
WarningsDo not increase cutter depth too much at once to avoid cutting copper strands; always stop the machine before changing parameters
Tagstrial cut / cutter depth adjustment / wire stripper

Step 2

Adjust Cutter Depth to Prevent Core Breakage

00:00:34

Training Card

Step NameAdjust Cutter Depth to Prevent Core Breakage
Why do this?Adjust the cutter depth value to prevent cutting internal copper wires during stripping.
When is this needed?When copper wires are found broken at the front strip after stripping.
Operation Steps
  1. Observe the stripped cable.
  2. Check copper wire condition on both half-strip and full-strip ends.
  3. Press the cutter depth key to access the parameter.
  4. Decrease the current cutter depth value.
  5. Press the confirm key to save the new value.
Success CheckAfter stripping again, no copper wires are broken, and the insulation is cut cleanly.
Common Mistakes
  • Adjusted only one side and forgot the other.
  • Pressed a key but did not press confirm to save.
  • Increased the value too much, causing more breakage.
Key Values
  • Initial cutter depth: 146 (current setting)
  • Adjusted cutter depth: 144 or 143 (suitable value without breaking copper wires)
WarningsAlways press confirm after each adjustment, otherwise the parameter will not take effect and wire breakage may occur immediately.
TagsWire stripper / Cutter depth adjustment / Core breakage prevention
