How to Choose the Right Wire Stripping Machine for Your Production

The right machine depends on wire range, daily output, process details, and total budget.

1. Wire Diameter

Confirm the smallest and largest wire sizes, insulation material, and whether the wire is single-core, multi-core, soft, or hard.

2. Production Volume

Low-volume sample work may need a simple setup. Daily batch production usually needs automatic feeding, cutting, and stripping.

3. Process Requirements

Check whether you need full stripping, half stripping, short wire processing, middle stripping, or custom blade configuration.

4. Budget

Compare machine price together with blade life, roller wear, spare parts, operator time, and remote support.

Recommended Direction

If your factory processes repeated electronic wires every day, choose an automatic computer wire stripping machine first. For unusual wires, send samples before confirming.
